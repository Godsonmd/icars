Our Mission

At i Cars, we believe that renting a car should be simple and stress-free. Our mission is to empower you to make informed choices about your transportation needs, so you can enjoy your journey with confidence. We're committed to providing personalized rental options that cater to your unique travel requirements, preferences, and lifestyle.

Our Team

We're proud to have a diverse team of professionals who are passionate about travel and mobility. Our experienced rental agents, automotive experts, and customer service specialists work tirelessly to bring you the best car rental experience. Each member of our team brings unique expertise and creativity to the table, ensuring that our services are both efficient and enjoyable.

Our Technology

We harness the power of technology to make car rental seamless and personalized. We strive to promote eco-friendly transportation options that are good for you and the planet. We celebrate diversity and cater to a wide range of rental needs, from compact cars to luxury vehicles. We also aim to educate our community about the benefits of responsible travel, empowering you to make informed choices.

1. INFORMATION AND INITIAL RECITALS

  • The RENTER shall provide accurate personal details, including name, age, birthday, address, and any other requested information.

  • Payments shall follow the agreed stipulations and computations.

  • The rental fee is as specified in the agreement unless modified by mutual consent.
  • Payments must be made free of any deductions, taxes, or charges.
  • Late payments will incur a 12% legal interest per annum.
  • The COMPANY reserves the right to reclaim the vehicle if the RENTER fails to pay on time.
  • The RENTER consents to credit card reservations for estimated charges.
  • All charges are subject to final audit and correction.

3. GENERAL PROVISIONS ON RENTED VEHICLE

  • The rented vehicle includes tires, tools, equipment, accessories, and documents belonging to the COMPANY.
  • The vehicle is rented solely for personal use.
  • The COMPANY is not responsible for manufacturer defects.
  • The RENTER assumes full responsibility for vehicle selection and compliance with laws.
  • Any manufacturer warranty claims must be handled directly by the RENTER.

4. TERM

  • The vehicle must be returned in good condition on the due date or upon demand.
  • Early return does not entitle the RENTER to a refund for unused rental time.
  • Cancellation by the RENTER may result in forfeiture of fees and deposits.
  • Reservation cancellations result in forfeiture of the reservation fee.

5. RELEASE AND RETURN OF THE RENTED VEHICLE

  • The vehicle shall be returned in the same condition, including a full fuel tank.
  • Prior to rental, the RENTER must inspect and approve the vehicle.
  • The RENTER must return all original accessories, tools, and equipment.
  • Failure to return the vehicle on time without approval may lead to legal action.

6. INSPECTION OF THE RENTED VEHICLE

  • The COMPANY may inspect the vehicle at any time during business hours.
  • Inspection includes both exterior and interior components.

7. PROHIBITED USES OF THE VEHICLE

  • Carrying passengers or property for commercial purposes.
  • Towing vehicles or trailers.
  • Participating in motorsports.
  • Transporting illegal goods.
  • Driving under the influence of alcohol or drugs.
  • Driving without a valid license.
  • Allowing unauthorized persons to drive.
  • Allowing new or inexperienced drivers (less than one year of driving experience).

8. LIABILITY FOR LOSS OR ACCIDENT

  • The RENTER covers the first P5,000.00 in repair costs for any accident.
  • Damage due to abusive use is the RENTER's responsibility.
  • Lost accessories, tools, or equipment will be charged to the RENTER.
  • The COMPANY is not liable for claims beyond insurance coverage.
  • Accidents must be reported immediately.
  • Total vehicle loss incurs a charge of 40% of the car's value.

9. OWNERSHIP OF THE RENTED VEHICLE

  • The vehicle remains the property of the COMPANY.
  • Failure to comply with terms may result in repossession at the RENTER's expense.
  • The COMPANY may retrieve the vehicle for non-payment or contract violations.
